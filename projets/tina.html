Tinatamagochi

I made mockups of a tamagochi game, in 64px, for #lowrezjam.

The game is about listening to the story of Tina, a girl who needs company. As your relationship grows, you may understand her and connect together.

I never played with pixel art before. I really liked it.

Artworks by Charles Boury, 2016-2017.
