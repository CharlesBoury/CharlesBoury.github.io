Tina64px tamagochi

I made mockups of a tamaogochi game, in 64px. It was a contribution to the #lowrezjam event on itch.io.

The game is about listening to the story of Tina, a girl who need company. As your relationship grows, you may understand her and connect together.

I never played with pixel art before. I really liked it.

Artworks by Charles Boury, 2016-2017.
