Schmilblick

art direction

I designed the User Interface of a mobile quizz app.

Le Schmilblick offers a new perspective on serious games: players help associations of public interest get donations by playing for them. I'm always enthousiast about what games can do to help society, so I joined the project.

The app had to charm a very large age group. I tried to give a light and joyful User Interface, inviting to generosity and usefulness in daily acts.
