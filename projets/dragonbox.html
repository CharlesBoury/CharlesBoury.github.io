DragonBoxcute maths

I worked at WWTK on a mobile game teaching maths equations to 8 year olds, without them knowing it. I did interactive mockups and game animations as well as backgrounds, interfaces, logo, and web trailers.

I put a lot of care in animations, to convey on the clearest and most memorable way the mathematical sense of actions.

The game graphics had to be appealling for children under 10 years old.
